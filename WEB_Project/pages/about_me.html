About me

Hello and welcome to my fursuit photography website! My nickname is AtroxTheFox, and I am a passionate photographer with a deep love for the furry community.

I have been a member of the furry community for several years, and I have always been drawn to the creativity and self-expression that fursuits allow. As a photographer, I am committed to capturing the unique spirit of each fursuit and the character it represents, and I strive to deliver good-quality photographs that my clients will treasure for years to come.

In addition to my passion for photography, I also have a strong interest in IT and electronics. I study computer science on high-school and I am always looking for new ways to incorporate technology into my photography work. This allows me to offer a wide range of services and options to my clients, mainly including post-processing techniques.

If you are interested in commissioning me as a fursuit photographer, please feel free to browse my portfolio and contact me to discuss your needs and ideas. I am always happy to answer any questions and I look forward to working with you to create stunning photographs that capture the unique spirit of your fursuit and character. Thank you for considering me as your fursuit photographer!
